The dataset contains financial market data including stock indices, volatility, treasury yields, commodities, and currency exchange rates. The data includes daily trading information with open, high, low, close prices and trading volumes for multiple financial instruments: S&P 500 (GSPC), NASDAQ (IXIC), VIX volatility index (VIX), 10-year Treasury yields (TNX), Gold futures (GC_F), Crude oil futures (CL_F), and US Dollar Index (DX-Y_NYB). This multivariate time series captures the complex interactions between equity markets, bond markets, commodities, and currency markets.
